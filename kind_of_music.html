Kind of Music (2011-2017)

Kind of Music is my personal blog during 2011-2017. In this blog, I wrote about (mostly) independent music in Taiwan and Japan. I tried to write about Taiwanese music in Japanese and also the other way around. Sometimes I wrote in both languages as well. I wrote less and less after I started my study for Master's degree and eventually stopped writing. Because of this blog, I met musicians and people who work in the music industry, and that was a very interesting experience.
