Bio

Yenchin Lai is a PhD candidate at the University of Bremen of Germany in Digital Media Lab, led by Prof. Dr. Rainer Malaka. She is a part of the Graduate School “Empowering Digital Media” funded by Klaus Tschira Foundation. Yenchin's research interests are in the field of Human-computer interacion (HCI). With her previous experience on Electrical Muscle Stimulation (EMS) in UIST'16 Student Innovation Contest and education in Geist project, led by Associate Professor Kai Kunze at Keio Media Design of Japan, she continues her work on Affective Computing and Everyday Interaction. Before she started her research life in HCI, she recieved her Bachelor's degree in Human Development and Family Studies at National Taiwan Normal University of Taiwan. During her spare time, she loves playing Nintendo Games, especially The Legend of Zelda.
